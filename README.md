🏥 Smart AI-Powered Health Monitoring System

📌 Overview

Smart AI-Powered Health Monitoring System is a C++-based healthcare assistant designed to help users monitor, understand, and improve their daily health habits through a single integrated platform.

This project addresses a common healthcare friction point: many people struggle to consistently manage essential health activities such as staying hydrated, maintaining healthy sleep schedules, remembering medications, and monitoring symptoms. These small but important habits are often neglected until health problems become more serious.

To help address this challenge, the system combines multiple healthcare management tools including water tracking, sleep monitoring, medicine reminders, symptom analysis, health risk assessment, health scoring, AI-inspired health guidance, and daily health reporting.

Developed as a hackathon and portfolio project, this system demonstrates how technology can support preventive healthcare and encourage healthier lifestyles.

🎯 Hackathon Theme Alignment

Friction Points: Redesigning Systems That Don't Serve Us

Many individuals face everyday healthcare challenges such as:

- Forgetting to take medications
- Poor hydration habits
- Inadequate sleep schedules
- Ignoring symptoms until conditions worsen
- Lack of personal health awareness
- Limited access to simple health monitoring tools

Traditional healthcare systems are often reactive, meaning people seek help only after problems become serious.

This project aims to redesign that experience by providing users with a simple healthcare assistant that promotes preventive healthcare, awareness, and healthier daily habits.

🧠 Health Score Logic

The system begins with a health score of 100.

Health score is adjusted based on:

- Water intake habits
- Sleep duration
- Reported symptoms

Based on the final score:

Score Range| Risk Level
80 - 100| Low Risk
50 - 79| Medium Risk
Below 50| High Risk

This allows users to quickly understand their overall health condition.
